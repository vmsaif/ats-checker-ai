Shibori Ltd. - Architect Recruitment

Shibori Ltd., a leading interior design and architecture firm, is seeking a talented and motivated architect to join our team. We are looking for someone with a passion for design, strong technical skills, and the ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
Responsibilities:

Develop creative and innovative design solutions for a variety of projects.
Prepare detailed construction documents, including drawings, specifications, and schedules.
Coordinate with clients, consultants, and contractors throughout the design and construction process.
Research and evaluate materials and systems for project implementation.
Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the field.
Qualifications:

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Architecture from an accredited program.
Strong 3D visualization skills and proficiency in relevant software.
Good understanding of Bill of Quantities (BOQ) preparation and materials selection.
At least 1 year of experience in a professional setting, but freshers are encouraged to apply.
Excellent communication and presentation skills.
Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite.
Bonus Qualifications:

Experience with sustainable design principles and practices.
Knowledge of local building codes and regulations.
Familiarity with project management software.
Benefits:

Competitive salary and benefits package.
Opportunities for professional development and growth.
Challenging and rewarding work environment.
Chance to contribute to exciting and innovative projects.
To Apply:

Please submit your resume, portfolio, and a cover letter outlining your qualifications and interest in the position to shiboricareer@gmail.com
Deadline for application: June 10, 2024

Shibori Ltd. is an equal opportunity employer committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace. We encourage all qualified candidates to apply.
