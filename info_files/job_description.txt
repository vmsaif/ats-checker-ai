What are we looking for?

We are looking for a Coding Instructor; an energetic individual with an engaging personality who believes in our philosophy of learning and innovating in a fun environment. Our coaches are the lifeblood of our operation. You will join our team of coaches to teach coding based on our proven curriculum.

Are you a technology teacher or do you have experience working with children? Do you enjoy teaching and building problem solving skills in young engineering minds? This could be the role for you.

Requirements

· Excellent oral communication.

· Upbeat, positive, energetic and works Independently.

· Experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and/or Java.

· Experience working with children age 6 to 15.

· Experience in science, technology and/or math will be preferred.

· Good listener and strong influencer.

· Ability to inspire and lead others.

· Troubleshoot and assist students with their programs in a high paced environment.

· Pre-employment background check is required.

Responsibilities include:

· Teach coding based on our proven curriculum.

· Create a positive and safe environment where innovation is encouraged.

· Track student progress and provide feedback to students and parents.
