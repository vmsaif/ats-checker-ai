**7. ATS Optimization Tips:**

* **Strategic Keyword Placement:** Incorporate keywords naturally within the "Skills" section, work experience descriptions (using the STAR method to showcase skills in past projects), and potentially even your resume summary.
* **Keyword Density:**  Avoid "keyword stuffing." Aim for a balance between keyword usage and natural, compelling language.
* **Tailoring and Customization:** Adapt your resume for each application, prioritizing keywords most relevant to the specific job description. 
* **Action Verbs & Numbers:**  Use action verbs to describe your accomplishments and quantify your impact whenever possible (e.g., "Led the design of a 10,000 sq ft office space..."). 
* **Portfolio:** Given the visual nature of the field, your portfolio is crucial.  Highlight projects that demonstrate the skills and experience outlined in the job description.
