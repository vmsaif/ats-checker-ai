**Job Description Summary**

**Role Title:** Artificial Intelligence Engineer

**Organization:** Radical AI for Social Good Lab (Radical AI)

**Key Responsibilities:**

* Design, develop, and enhance innovative AI models using machine learning, deep learning, generative AI, and natural language processing techniques
* Analyze and preprocess data for machine learning applications
* Collaborate with AI teams to optimize algorithms and models
* Present research findings and engage in critical discussions to advance AI capabilities
* Contribute to the development of AI virtual agents

**Essential Qualifications:**

* Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, or a related field
* Deep understanding of AI principles, machine learning algorithms, and natural language processing
* Proficiency in Python and AI development frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch)
* Strong analytical, problem-solving, and creative thinking abilities
* Excellent communication and teamwork skills

**Additional Information:**

* Unpaid virtual internship with a duration of 3 months, starting between March and July 2024
* Access to the Radical AI Platform and direct feedback from the Radical AI Core Team
* Technologies utilized include OpenAI API, Meta Llama 2, Google Gemini, LangChain, Google Gen AI Studio, and Python
* Applicants with diverse backgrounds and experiences are encouraged to apply
* Radical AI is committed to diversity, inclusion, and equal opportunity

**Resume Section Relevance:**

To determine which sections of an applicant's resume are most relevant to the role, recruiters should consider the following criteria:

* **Education:** Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, or a related field
* **Skills:** Proficiency in Python, AI development frameworks, machine learning, deep learning, generative AI, natural language processing, and data analysis
* **Experience:** Experience in designing, developing, and enhancing AI models and collaborating on AI projects
* **Awards and Recognitions:** Awards or recognition for achievements in AI research or development
* **Personal Traits and Interests:** Analytical mindset, problem-solving abilities, and passion for AI
* **Miscellaneous:** Participation in AI-related hackathons, conferences, or open-source projects
