**6. ATS Optimization Tips:**

* **Strategic Placement:** Incorporate the keywords naturally in your resume's:
    * **Summary/Objective:** Briefly highlight how your skills and experience align with the role and company mission. 
    * **Skills Section:** Create a dedicated skills section listing both hard and soft skills.
    * **Experience Descriptions:**  Use keywords when describing your previous roles and accomplishments, focusing on quantifiable results.

* **Keyword Density:** Use keywords strategically and avoid keyword stuffing. Focus on clear and concise language that accurately reflects your qualifications.

* **Tailoring and Customization:**  Adapt your resume content and keywords for each job application, highlighting the most relevant skills for the specific position.
